Our Company

We grow the reach and scale of our platform by distributing our operating system through our streaming players and licensing arrangements with TV OEMs and pay TV operators. We generate revenue from selling CatchOn TV1 streaming players in retail, through our licensing arrangements with TV OEMs and pay TV operators, and from content consumption, promotion and advertising sales on the platform.

We believe that all television will be streamed, and that all TVs and set-top-boxes will be powered by software-based operating systems that provide a dramatically better user experience than has existed historically for the TV. Like the computer and mobile markets, a similar dynamic is happening with the big screen TV where only a small number of highly competitive platforms will exist.

The Power of Streaming

CatchOn TV is on the path to pioneering streaming for the TV, and we aspire to power every TV in the world. We developed a streaming platform that delivers a best-in-class user experience. At the heart of the CatchOn TV platform is our proprietary operating system, the CatchOn OS,

which we built from the ground up. CatchOn TV is recognized for streaming innovation. This is demonstrated by our growing, passionate and highly engaged customer base who love CatchOn TV streaming devices because of our coveted content selection, ease of use, and value.
